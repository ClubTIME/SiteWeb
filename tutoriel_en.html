TUTORIALS

Sécurité Sociale (Ameli)

The Sécurité Sociale is a compulsory health coverage. Partial reimbursements, up to 70% of the medical costs incurred.

CAF

The CAF (Caisse d'Allocations Familiales) is the social security service responsible for the family. This organization pays aid such as housing allowance.

Mutuelle SMENO

The SMENO is the complementary health insurance which allows better reimbursement, but it is not compulsory. This cover will cover the part of the costs not covered by social security.

Carte Vitale

The Carte Vitale is a card issued in France to people contributing to a social security organization such as the Caisse Primaire d'Assurance Maladie. In particular, it makes it possible to certify rights to health insurance benefits.

Titre de Sejour

The Titre de Séjour is a secure document ensuring recognition by the public authority of the right to reside in the national territory for a foreign national of full age.
