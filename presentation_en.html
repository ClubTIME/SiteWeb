What is the Club TIME?

Hi new G1 (or G0 as everyone will call you for the next couple of months) Centrale Lille! The Club TIME welcomes you! We will be your first point of reference and we will give you all the information you need to start well this adventure. But who we are ?

The Club TIME is made up of people who want to make life easier for internationals in Lille and who are themselves interested in unknown cultures. This involves the international and French.  

We work in all possible areas: we organize convivial events like cheese tastings or running dinners (you will quickly get to know the concept, this is one of the best things at Centrale), we give additional lessons in the most difficult subjects and we organize trips. Do not hesitate to read the descriptions of the different poles for more information.

This website will allow you to form a first impression on Centrale Lille and on who will welcome you. Also you will find all the important data to take steps administrative tasks and to contact the persons responsible for asking for help.

The whole Club TIME is looking forward to getting to know you and we will welcome you all with open arms. See you soon !

Le Petit Bureau (PB)

Le petit bureau is the core of Club TIME. They are in contact with the school administration, with the respos of the different poles and they manage the planning and the finances of the Club. To best represent all members, it consists of a maximum of nationalities.

This year we have Alan as president (prez), who comes from Mexico. He organizes all the events with the help of our secretary (screz) Yishan, from China, our treasurer (trez) Simon, a handsome German, and the Brazilian Matheus as our vice-president (vice-prez). This office is extremely dynamic and ambitious and it will organize an incredible start for you. You will find their contacts at the bottom of the page and they will gladly answer all your concerns. Don't hesitate, that's what they are here for.

The Poles

Communication

The comm (communication) pole is responsible for the visibility of Club TIME. That means they manages social media, advertises to attract new members, and designs club clothing. If you like the expression creative or if you just have a few recommendations, Ting and Will, our respos, are here for you.

Event

The event pole takes care of the organization of all festive, cultural and integrative events. Some examples are theater outings, running dinners / cocktails, game evenings or tastings of different specialties. Our respos this year are Pablo A. and Yuzhe. They are open to all your proposals and invite you to make the most of all possible events. Participating will help you meet new people and to discover all the cultures represented in the Club TIME.      

Travel

The pole which was quite small the year before. That’s why everyone is so keen on taking some amazing trips next year. Cecilia and Manuel are our respos who will make you discover your destinations dream. Whether it's the beach, Paris, Brussels or even England, the most important thing is that you are ready to have fun and discover new places! We all knew that Lille is perfectly positioned for discover a lot of European countries. Why not enjoy it ?

Studies

Unfortunately Centrale Lille is not Centrale Vacances throughout the year. Every now and then there are exams to get and we all had our difficulties with that. This is why we have the studies center which will help you with additional lessons in the most difficult subjects. It really makes a difference and we are very happy to have enough volunteers to be able to offer you this opportunity. Do not be afraid of the confrontation with the teachers of the common core, we are here to strengthen you.

Master

This is the first year of the master's pole and we hope to develop it strongly. The path for students coming for the master's is different from that of double degrees, but that doesn't mean we don't have anything in common. Nina is the respo of this pole and the entire Club TIME will be grateful for any individual who wishes to join or only participate in events.
